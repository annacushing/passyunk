  • SOUTH PHILLY BARBACOA

    Specializing in slow-cooked lamb tacos three days a week, from 5 a.m. until everything sells out, South Philly Barbacoa offers some of the best lamb barbacoa and pancita tacos in the city.

  • MARRA’S

    Founded by Neapolitan Immigrants, this old-school Italian spot has been dishing up red-sauce-covered plates and brick-oven pizzas since 1927. Bringing back the hood’s Italian American roots with huge veal parms and garlicky clams with linguine.

    $$1734 EAST PASSYUNK

  • MELROSE DINER

    This neighborhood diner makes for a classic experience whether it’s the start of a day or the end of a night. Come here for all American eats and people-watching opportunities.

  • PARADISO

    The beginning of Passyunk’s renaissance can be accredited to the creation of this Italian fine dining by owner Lynn Rinaldi. As a bonus to the food, Paradiso also features fresh rooftop-grown produce and weekly live jazz.

    Eat pastrami-and-Swiss bao buns, maple syrup matzo-meal turnip cakes, and soup dumplings at this nontraditional dim sum parlor. Hangout as inventive Asian cocktails come by the glass or pitcher.

    $$1648 EAST PASSYUNK

  • ULTIMO COFFEE
